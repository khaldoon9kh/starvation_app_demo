Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how GRC Starvation Toolkit ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: GRC Starvation Toolkit is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All app data is stored locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The GRC Starvation Toolkit app operates entirely offline and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details.

The only data the app handles includes:

  • Downloaded Content: Medical reference materials, diagrams, templates, and educational content that you download for offline use. This content is stored locally on your device.
  • Saved Items: Articles, templates, or diagrams you choose to save within the app are stored locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Your language preference and other settings are stored locally on your device.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data to use. The locally stored content is used solely to:

  • Provide you with offline access to medical reference materials
  • Remember your saved items and preferences
  • Improve your user experience within the app

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using standard mobile storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data, and it never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it (e.g., saving a diagram to your photo library).

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Used solely for downloading and syncing educational content. No personal user data is collected or transmitted through Firebase. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Expo: The app is built using Expo framework. Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and usage statistics to improve their services. See Expo Privacy Policy.

5. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Photo Library Access: Only requested if you choose to save diagrams or templates to your device's photo library. This is optional and initiated by you.
  • Internet Access: Required to download and update educational content from our servers.

You can manage these permissions through your device's settings at any time.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to outside parties. Since we don't collect personal data, there is nothing to share.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app does not specifically target children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app contains medical educational content intended for healthcare professionals and students.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a device passcode or biometric lock
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Not sharing your device with untrusted individuals

9. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored on your device and accessible to you
  • Deletion: You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings
  • Portability: Saved images can be exported to your photo library

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

11. Compliance

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Google Play Store requirements
  • Apple App Store requirements
